Stream or download audiobooks hosted on your Plex server with Chronicle, the best Android Audiobook Player for Plex.

<b>📚 Key Features</b>

• <b>Sync Progress</b> - Keep your audiobook progress synchronized across your devices
• <b>Multiple Formats</b> - Support for mp3, m4a, and m4b audiobook files
• <b>Adjustable Playback Speed</b> - Listen at your own pace with customizable playback speeds
• <b>Auto-Rewind</b> - Never lose your place with automatic rewind on resume
• <b>Sleep Timer</b> - Fall asleep to your favorite books with built-in sleep timer
• <b>Skip Silence</b> - Automatically skip silent portions for efficient listening
• <b>Download Books</b> - Download audiobooks for offline playback anytime, anywhere
• <b>Android Auto Support</b> - Basic Android Auto support for safe playback while driving

<b>📖 Perfect for Plex Users</b>

Chronicle is designed specifically for Plex users who want a dedicated audiobook player with features tailored for long-form content. Connect to your Plex server and enjoy your entire audiobook collection with an interface optimized for audiobooks.

<b>🎯 Requirements</b>

• A Plex server with audiobook content
• Android 13 or higher
• Network access to your Plex server
• For Android Auto: you must be logged into the app before you are able to use it in your vehicle
